Retention

Lesson guide

What this role is: a YouTube creator plans videos, writes titles, chooses thumbnails, edits for retention, and publishes consistently.

How the platform works: YouTube rewards videos people click and keep watching. Strong watch time, clear topic, and useful structure help the channel grow.

What to learn first: channel layout, uploads, title writing, thumbnail basics, watch time, retention, descriptions, tags, and community posts.

A simple content flow: pick one topic, write a clear promise, make the thumbnail match the promise, deliver the answer fast, then end with a next step.

Common mistakes: clickbait titles that do not match the video, noisy thumbnails, long intros, and uploading without a clear audience.
