Ticketing and Resolution

Support work becomes manageable when every issue has a ticket, a clear owner, a follow-up, and a final resolution.

Ticketing

Capture the issue, the request, and the owner in one place.

Forwarding

Send the issue to the right team when you cannot solve it directly.

Follow-up

Check the ticket again until the customer gets a clear answer.
