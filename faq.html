Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to the questions clients usually ask before they request a proposal or compare agency options.

What services can you bundle into one monthly engagement?

We can combine strategy and execution across social media management, paid social marketing, technical SEO, blog strategy, web design, web development, site management, catalogue operations, and customer support workflows. Each proposal is shaped around your actual priorities instead of a one-size-fits-all package.

Do you handle both design and technical implementation?

Yes. We cover design direction, UX structure, front-end execution, dynamic builds, e-commerce builds, and ongoing technical management. That makes it easier to keep brand, performance, and conversion goals aligned.

Can you work as an external growth team instead of a one-off vendor?

Yes. Many clients bring us in as a long-term operating partner for recurring execution, reporting, optimization, catalogue updates, or support operations. We can also start with a focused project and expand from there.

How do you price social, technical SEO, blog, and website work?

Pricing depends on scope, timelines, number of channels, site complexity, publishing frequency, technical audit depth, reporting needs, support hours, and whether you need ongoing management or a fixed-deliverable project. The quote form below helps us price accurately instead of guessing.

Do you create blogs for Google, Bing, ChatGPT, Gemini, and AI search?

Yes. We build blog programs that support classic search engines like Google and Bing while also improving discoverability for AI-assisted engines and answer surfaces such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and other search experiences that rely on strong site structure and useful content.

Do you support e-commerce catalogues and store operations?

Yes. We can support catalogue uploads, attribute cleanup, product organization, merchandising flow, storefront updates, and related operational support for e-commerce growth.

Can you help with voice and non-voice customer support?

Yes. We support customer operations across voice, non-voice, chat, email, and workflow design. If you need quality standards, escalation handling, or structured support systems, we can include that in the quote.

What should I prepare before asking for a quote?

The most helpful details are your business type, current goals, rough timeline, existing website or social links, and which services you want us to handle. If you do not have everything ready yet, just tell us what stage you are in and we can guide the next step.
